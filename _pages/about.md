I am **Hao Zhang (张浩)**, a Ph.D. candidate in Traffic Information Engineering and
Control at **Shanghai Maritime University**, and an engineer at the **CMA
Meteorological Routeing Centre** (China Meteorological Administration), where I help
lead operational ship weather routeing services and R&D for ocean-going vessels.

My work focuses on **intelligent ships** and **ship weather routeing**, combining
marine meteorology with route optimization to make ocean voyages safer and more
efficient — including tropical-cyclone avoidance, sailing-risk assessment, ship
speed-loss prediction, and Arctic navigation.

Research Interests
======

- Tropical cyclone avoidance technology
- Ship sailing risk assessment

- Marine meteorological services
