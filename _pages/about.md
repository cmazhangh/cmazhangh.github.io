I am **Hao Zhang**, a Ph.D. candidate in Traffic Information Engineering and
Control at **Shanghai Maritime University** and an engineer at the **CMA
Meteorological Routeing Centre** of the China Meteorological Administration.
My work sits at the intersection of **marine meteorology** and **intelligent
navigation**. I develop weather-aware route-optimization methods that help ships
avoid tropical cyclones, limit speed loss in heavy seas, and sail more safely and
efficiently — from busy commercial lanes to the emerging Arctic passages. I am
equally at home turning these methods into reliable, day-to-day forecasting
services for the shipping industry.

Research Interests
======
- Ship weather routeing and route optimization
- Tropical cyclone avoidance for ships
- Sailing-risk assessment and navigation safety
- Ship speed-loss prediction from marine-meteorological data
- Marine meteorological services for shipping
- Arctic navigation risk forecasting
